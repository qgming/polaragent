Opening

The magazine template is designed for narrative-driven content. It combines a large serif headline, italic deck, and editorial typography to create a reading experience that feels substantial and engaging.

Use this style for long-form features, thought leadership pieces, profile articles, industry analysis, or any document where storytelling matters as much as information. The generous type scale and rhythmic spacing reward sustained reading.

The Narrative Arc

Magazine-style documents are organized around a central narrative arc rather than a hierarchical outline. Each section advances the story, building toward a conclusion or insight that feels earned rather than stated.

"A great feature makes the reader feel smarter for having read it, not just better informed."

In practice, this means leading with anecdote or observation, building context gradually, and reserving judgment for the climax. The structure should feel inevitable but not predictable.
