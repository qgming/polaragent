Why this work now

Proposal Summary

Meridian's intake process is ready for a practical redesign: the current workflow is understood, the highest-friction steps are measurable, and frontline teams have a clear appetite for fewer handoffs.

Reduce avoidable rework

Standardize capture logic and validation before information reaches operations teams.

Improve patient experience

Remove duplicate questions and make completion status visible across channels.

Create rollout discipline

Deliver a playbook that can be reused clinic by clinic without redesigning the program.

Included

Scope of Work

  • Current-state workflow mapping and intake data audit.
  • Future-state service blueprint and approval-ready process design.
  • Prototype intake experience with validation rules and staff review states.
  • Implementation roadmap, pilot metrics, and rollout governance model.
Boundaries

Out of Scope

  • Production software build beyond prototype screens and technical specification.
  • Medical record system customization or direct system integration work.
  • Legal review of consent language, privacy terms, or clinical policy.
  • Training delivery outside the pilot leadership and operations teams.
